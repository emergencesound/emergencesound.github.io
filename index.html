Recording

Emergence Sound employs a highly trained engineering staff that is dedicated to capturing the best recording possible for your music or voiceover project. We are well-versed in professional recording techniques, microphone selection/placement and room acoustics. Recording rates are $55/song if the song is recorded within 2 hours. If 2 hours is exceeded, a rate of $40/hour will be charged for the studio time.

Mixing

After recording, it's time to mix! We use a wide array of professional plugins, mixing modules and good old fashioned listening to make sure your mix is just right. If you have a preferred compression, EQ, or time-based effect plugin let us know and we can accommodate. We also offer remote mixing services for clients that are not local. Mixing rates are $30/hour.

Mastering

Emergence Sound carefully uses the iZotope Ozone 5 Mastering Suite to add that extra radio play glisten to your tracks. For more information about the art of mastering, feel free to visit this page. Emergence Sound charges a flat rate of $20/song for mastering.

Editing

Have an audio file you need edited or digitally restored? Send it our way and we can make it happen! Emergence Sound is equipped with excellent digital audio editing and restoration software we can use to save your sound. Audio Editing rates are $30/hour.
